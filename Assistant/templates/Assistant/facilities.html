Facilities at Poornima Institute of Engineering & Technology

It’s a part of academics where all facilities are provided with updated technologies and equipments. The significance of R&D can be highlighted as: To promote research and development activities in the institution. To equip the students and the faculty members with latest advancement in the technological areas. To provide engineering consultancy and research in innovative areas. To enhance the engineering education with research orientation fulfilling the technical needs of industries. Machine Drawing Lab, Physics Lab, Computer Lab, Language Lab for the 1st year department. Online examination lab is located on the ground oor of the new building. IBM Lab for research on Business Intelligence and Cloud Computing is located on the second floor of the Engineering block.

The PIET Central Library supports the teaching and research programmes of the Institute and provides facilities for general reading and disseminates information according to the requirement of the users The Library is an organized collection of the source of information and similar made accessible to a defined branch for reference or borrowing. It provides physical material and digital access to the material and physical building. PIET library’s collection included book, periodicals, newspapers CD.DVD and Other formats are a primary resource for getting information. The mission of PIET library and Learning Resources Center is to support the Institute mission in identifying, organizing, preserving and offering accessible resources which serve the needs of college Faculty/staff members and students at large. In addition, the library seeks to locate, acquire, organize and select the most appropriate material and make it accessible to the users. It is also the mission of the library to build a comprehensive, balanced library collection and provide a good environment for reading, learning, and research. The upgrading and preserving of the library’s information technology infrastructure to ensure prompt access to information and information services are also among the PIET library mission priorities. The PIET Library provides a welcoming learning environment for students and staff, not only supports the College’s aims and curriculum also encourages the love of reading and the development of students’ independent learning skills.

The most earning weather of all the feathers of PF block is ARBUDA CONVENTION CENTRE located at PIET Campus. It is a well air-conditioned auditorium having a seating capacity of over 500 people. It is equipped with the fully operational technical advancement of multimedia and graphics & sound proof audiovideo facilities. All the presentations, seminars, conferences and other pragmatic plans are organized here. Altogether, almost all the essential academic and nonacademic activities / events are organized in this auditorium which enlivens the audience, organizers, and the participants as well. There is an examination hall above the auditorium which also comprises of 6 group discussion rooms and 3 Personal Interview rooms which make possible the ideal setting for carrying out various examinations and conduction of different training and placement sessions for the final and pre-final year candidates.
